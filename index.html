Okay Reader

Okay reader is simple, clean and modern windows phone 8.1 news reader. Able to retrieve news either directly from RSS feed or using Feedly account.

Main features
- works in offline
- sync in background
- read articles inside the application
- integration with feedly services
- save pictures from the article to your phone
- ability to disable images to keep your traffic

Windows 10 Windows Phone 8.1 and earlier
